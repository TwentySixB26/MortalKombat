Turnament Kombat

The Kombat Tournament is a game tournament held by NetherRealm Studios. This tournament is divided into two phases, namely the group phase and the knockout phase. Each person has the right to choose one of the characters in the game Mortal Kombat 11. The prize itself is 1st place $7000, 2nd place $5000 and 3rd place $3000.
