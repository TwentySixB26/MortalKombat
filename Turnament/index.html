Tournament Kombat

The Kombat Tournament is a game tournament held by NetherRealm Studios. This tournament is divided into two phases, namely the group phase and the knockout phase. Each person has the right to choose one of the characters in the game Mortal Kombat 11. The prize itself is 1st place $7000, 2nd place $5000 and 3rd place $3000.

Fase Grup

1 group consists of 4 people and each person has to win 2 times if they want to get perfect points, here there are no character bans and those in 3rd and 4th place will fall.

KnockOut

Players who lose twice will be considered disqualified and will not be able to advance to the next round. Here, the character ban also applies to this round, a total of two characters will be banned.
