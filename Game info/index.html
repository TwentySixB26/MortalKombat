Mortal Kombat 11

Mortal Kombat is a fighting genre game made by NetherRealm Studios, this game tells the story of leaders in one realm fighting against each other to conquer another realm in order to expand their realm, but in the Mortal Kombat 11 series the story will focus on chronicle of the titan who wants to turn back time so that raiden the elder god will never be united with liu kang, because according to him the combo of raiden and liukang is very overpowered and the world is not fair, because according to him a just world is when the sides of light and darkness are very strong and in the end they fight each other and it will never be finished.

Story Mode

In Mortal Kombat 11 there are two parts, namely the first series Mortal Kombat 11 and the second series Aftermath. Both parts are included in the story mode which is quite exciting.

Customize

You can use customize to change the skin of one of the characters, you can also use it to change the custom color of clothes, weapons or fighting equipment owned by one of the characters.

Fight Mode

Fight Mode is a one versus one part, fighting like a true fighter who relies on basic attacks and the skills possessed by each character, because each character has their own skills.

Friendship

Friendship can occur in fight mode when you play in fight mode and you defeat your enemy then you can finish your opponent and this friendship can occur.
